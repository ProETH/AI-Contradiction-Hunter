# AI Contradiction Hunter

AI Contradiction Hunter is an explainable AI Trading Skill developed for the CoinW AI Trading Skill Challenge.

Unlike traditional trading systems that search only for bullish or bearish signals, AI Contradiction Hunter identifies conflicts between independent market evidence before allowing an AI Agent to execute a trade.

The philosophy is simple:

> Markets become dangerous when evidence disagrees.

Instead of asking:

"Should I Buy?"

AI Contradiction Hunter asks:

"Can this evidence be trusted?"

---

# Why AI Contradiction Hunter?

Many trading losses occur because traders focus only on positive signals while ignoring conflicting market evidence.

For example:

- Price rises
- Volume increases
- Funding remains positive

Meanwhile:

- Whales are selling
- Capital Commitment is weakening
- Open Interest is decreasing

Most trading systems still generate a BUY signal.

AI Contradiction Hunter detects these conflicts before a decision is made.

# Why It Matters

AI Contradiction Hunter does not predict price.

It evaluates whether available evidence agrees or conflicts before an AI Agent commits capital.

The objective is to reduce false-positive trading signals and improve decision quality.
